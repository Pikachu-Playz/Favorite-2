Shrenik Daripkar's Favorite Thing To Do

Origami

Origami is the art of folding paper. You can do it by folding paper into shapes and objects that can just be models or can move. Paper airplanes are origamis too. Yoshizawa Akira was the person who created origami. Origami helps develop hand-eye coordination, good motor skills, and mental concentration.
